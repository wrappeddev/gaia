GAIA CLAN GLOBAL

Welcome to GAIA Clan Global

Established in 2010

WHO ARE WE?

GAIA Clan Global is an official online community for people looking for companionship and a loyal place to call family. With a long history of outstanding service in the Xbox gaming community, GAIA has since expanded to multiple platforms. We've moved most of our operations to Discord, a great platform for people to come together, play games, and meet others. Here, we play on a variety of platforms: PC, Xbox, PlayStation, Nintendo, VR, and more!

GAIA CLAN ORIGINS

GAIA Clan Global started as VENM, a successful online gaming community on console before deciding to change to a moniker that more closely resembled our family-oriented ideals. We're named after Mother Earth, GAIA, and like a mother should, we strive to take care of our family and be a benefit to our communities. We aim to uphold standards that anyone could admire and encourage helping our fellow man. At the end of the day, however, we value our own above all. "Greater love has no one but this: to lay down one's life for one's friends."

GAIA Clan Global is more than just a group, it's a history deep and rich with people and events. There are too many aspects to even begin listing them all. There are countless layers of old history, but what about the new history? People like you are the people that create new history.

Join Us!

Ready to become part of our community? Joining GAIA Clan is simple! Visit our Discord server and follow the instructions in the welcome channel to get started on your journey with us.

Our Activities

From casual gaming sessions to competitive tournaments, movie nights to voice chat hangouts, GAIA Clan offers a variety of activities for members to enjoy together as a community.
